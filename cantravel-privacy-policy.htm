CAN TRAVEL

PRIVACY POLICY

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Can Travel (“Application”) for mobile devices. The Application is an automatic travel journal that logs trips and tracks your physical presence within Canadian border. 

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

User Provided Information 

The Application obtains and store on your device the information you provide when you setup your profile or manually log a trip outside Canada.

When you setup your profile with the application, you provide (a) the date you became Permanent Resident of Canada, and; (b) date you became a Temporary Resident of Canada if any. When you log your trips, you typically provide (c) dates of the trip, (d) country visited and (e) the reason of the trip.

All provided information is saved locally in your device memory. We don’t transmit, collect, share, backup this information or use it in any way other than to perform necessary calculation locally within the application.

Automatically Collected Information 

If you opt-in to automatic trip logging, the Application obtains your device approximate location from time to time. The location data is processed locally on your device, the Application does not transmit your location data anywhere to process. All logged trips are saved locally on your device.

Information Collected from Location History

You may choose to automatically import you past trips from Google Location History. The location history is processed locally on your device using a file you download from Google. The Application does not transmit your location history anywhere to process, does not store, share or use your location history in any way other than to locally log your trips outside Canadian border.

Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?

No. The information collected by the Application does not leave your device.  

Data Retention Policy, Managing Your Information

We do not collect or retain your data anywhere apart from your device memory. Your data will be lost if you uninstall the application or reset your phone. We can not recover your data or transfer it to a new device.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason. You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes, as continued use is deemed approval of all changes.
