CAN TRAVEL

PRIVACY POLICY

Effective date: 26 July 2020

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Can Travel (“Application”) for mobile devices. The Application is an automatic travel journal that logs trips and tracks your physical presence within Canadian border. 

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

User Provided Information 

The Application obtains and store on your device the information you provide when you setup your profile or manually log a trip outside Canada.

When you setup your profile with the application, you provide (a) the date you became Permanent Resident of Canada, and; (b) date you became a Temporary Resident of Canada if any. When you log your trips, you typically provide (c) dates of the trip, (d) country visited and (e) the reason of the trip.

All provided information is saved locally in your device memory. We don’t transmit, collect, share, backup this information or use it in any way other than to perform necessary calculation locally within the application.

Automatically Collected Information 

We collect some anonymous information about your usage of the Application, namely the following: your device ID; your device ad ID; application instance ID, your IP address; first application start time; profile completion time; status of the automatic trip logger (enabled/disabled); start and finish of location history import process, its completion status and the number of trips logged as the result; manual trip log time; total number of logged trips; start and finish time of Physical Presence Calculator auto-fill process, its completion status and total number of filled trips. We do not collect or retain any personal information such as dates in the user profile, dates of the trips, visited countries or reason of the trips. We do not collect any information you entered in the Physical Presence Calculator web-page during auto-filling process.

If you opt-in for the automatic trip logging, the Application obtains your device approximate location from time to time. The location data is processed locally on your device, the Application does not transmit your location data anywhere to process. All logged trips are saved locally on your device.

Information Collected from Location History

You may choose to automatically import you past trips from Google Location History. The location history is processed locally on your device using a file you download from Google. The Application does not transmit your location history anywhere to process, does not store, share or use your location history in any way other than to locally log your trips.

How do we use collected app usage information

We use collected usage information to make the application more convenient to use and for debug purposes. We also use collected events from time to time to increase perfomance of our Google Ad campaings.

Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?

The information about your usage of the Application is collected using Google Analytics for Firebase and Microsoft App Center services. The access to the data is governed by these companies’ respective privacy policies.

Data Retention Policy, Managing Your Information

We do not collect or retain your personal data anywhere except for your device memory. Your data will be lost if you uninstall the application or reset your phone. We can not recover your data or transfer it to a new device at this point of time.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason. You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes, as continued use is deemed approval of all changes.
